## 📖 Overview

<a href="#sweetHome3D">Sweet Home 3D</a> serves homeowners planning renovations or furniture purchases, renters visualizing how their space could be arranged, real estate professionals creating property visualizations, and design enthusiasts exploring interior design without the cost of professional software.

The <a href="#sweetHome3D">homeowner renovation planning use case</a> uses Sweet Home 3D to visualize renovation possibilities before committing budget — testing different room layouts, furniture arrangements, and structural changes virtually before making physical changes or furniture purchases. The free cost removes the barrier that commercial design software's pricing creates for occasional personal use. The <a href="#sweetHome3D">furniture shopping and arrangement use case</a> applies the accurate measurement tools to verify that planned furniture purchases will fit and look appropriate in the actual space — modeling a room with precise dimensions and testing furniture placement before buying.

The <a href="#sweetHome3D">real estate and rental visualization use case</a> creates floor plans and 3D visualizations for property listings, helping potential buyers or renters understand a space's layout and potential furniture arrangement before viewing in person. <a href="#sweetHome3D">Educational and hobbyist use</a> makes Sweet Home 3D valuable for students learning interior design concepts and hobbyists exploring design as a creative interest without professional software investment.</p>

<a href="#sweetHome3D">Open-source development model</a> means Sweet Home 3D is developed transparently with community involvement, ensuring the application remains free and continues receiving updates from an engaged development community rather than depending on a single commercial entity's continued investment. <a href="#sweetHome3D">Cross-platform availability</a> on Mac, Windows, and Linux ensures design files and skills transfer regardless of the computer being used, useful for users who work across multiple platforms or share projects with others on different operating systems.

<a href="#sweetHome3D">SH3D file format and import/export</a> supports saving projects in Sweet Home 3D's native format alongside import of additional furniture models and export of plans to common formats for sharing or printing. <a href="#sweetHome3D">Active user community</a> contributes additional furniture catalogs, textures, and design resources beyond the built-in library, expanding the available design elements for specific style preferences or regional furniture styles.</p>

## 🤔 FAQ

<details>
<summary>Is prior design experience required for Sweet Home 3D?</summary>

No. Sweet Home 3D is designed to be accessible to users without professional design training, with intuitive drag-and-drop furniture placement.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Can Sweet Home 3D handle precise architectural measurements?</summary>

Yes. The floor plan tools support precise numerical input for exact wall lengths, room dimensions, and furniture placement.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Does Sweet Home 3D support custom textures and materials?</summary>

Yes. Texture and material customization can be applied to walls, floors, and furniture for more realistic visualization.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Is Sweet Home 3D actively maintained?</summary>

Yes. As an open-source project, Sweet Home 3D receives ongoing updates from its development community.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I work on multiple design projects at once?</summary>

Yes. Multiple project support allows working on or comparing several design plans simultaneously.

</details>

<details>
<summary>What file format does Sweet Home 3D use?</summary>

Sweet Home 3D uses its native SH3D format for saving projects, with import and export options for sharing and printing plans.

</details>
